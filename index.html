Haleigh Wright - She/Her/Hers

I combine my technical, research, and business expertise to help improve livelihoods through technology.

With seven years of interdisciplinary education and nearly four years of research experience, I thrive in interdicsiplinary settings. I believe that the best solutions result from people with different experiences coming together to reach a common goal. Everything is connected, and I am passionate about innovating, connecting, and translating complicated technological solutions to the business strategies and outcomes they can help improve.

I am a recent Technology Management MS and Computer Science MS graduate from the University of California, Santa Barbara, and an aspiring consultant. I pursued my BS in Scientific Computing (half math, half computer science) with a concentration in Environmental Science from the University of Pittsburgh in 2011 after graduating high school early to jumpstart my education and career. After recieving my BS in 2015, I went directly to UCSB to complete my graduate work. Throughout my education, I took on leadership roles including Business Manager of the CS honor society Upsilon Pi Epsilon, Women in Computer Science Co-President, CS Graduate Representatives President, and CS Diversity Committee Student Chair.

In my free time, you can find me near a body of water reading, writing poetry, or oil painting.
